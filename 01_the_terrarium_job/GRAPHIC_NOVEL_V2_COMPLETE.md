## Story Themes

### 1. Accessibility of Creation
Modern tools democratize entrepreneurship. Alex goes from idea to revenue in hours, not months.

### 2. Human + AI Partnership
Not AI replacing humans, but amplifying human creativity. Build enables, Alex creates.

### 3. Infrastructure as Superpower
WooCommerce provides the operating system. Alex focuses on product and customers.

### 4. Learning Systems
The platform learns Alex's business and provides increasingly sophisticated insights.

### 5. Infinite Iteration
Success doesn't end the story - it enables the next one. The cycle continues.
