About This Story

The Terrarium Job follows Alex, who wakes up with an idea for modular terrariums and launches a live store the same day with Woo's help. By the end of eight months, they're running a thriving business with employees and subscribers.

This is a vision story—an exploration of what's possible when AI assistance and commerce infrastructure get out of your way. It imagines a future where the only barrier between idea and revenue is the quality of your idea itself.
