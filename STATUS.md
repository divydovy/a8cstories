## Key Learnings

### What Worked in V2

âœ… **Multi-panel layouts** - Proper comic book pacing
âœ… **Character dialogue** - Shows personality and relationship dynamics
âœ… **Maya addition** - Grounds the story, audience surrogate
âœ… **Speech bubbles** - Traditional format makes it feel like a real comic
âœ… **Emotional progression** - Joy, doubt, validation, growth visible through dialogue
âœ… **Circular narrative** - Page 14-15 callback to Page 1 provides closure

### Technical Approach

- Keep prompts focused on panel content, not overly detailed
- Specify character consistency in each prompt
- Use clear panel descriptions (top wide, middle split, etc.)
- Neo-noir aesthetic works beautifully for comic book format
- Gemini handles multi-panel layouts surprisingly well

### Narrative Structure

- 15 pages provides good arc with breathing room
- 3-4 panels per page = natural reading rhythm
- Character introduction timing matters (Maya at midpoint perfect)
- External validation (reviews, Maya's perspective) crucial for credibility
- Epilogue panel provides perfect punctuation
